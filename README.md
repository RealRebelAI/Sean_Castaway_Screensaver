# Sean_Castaway_Screensaver
a simple clone and upgrade of Johnny Castaway. (uses my dads likeness) simply for my father as a gift but your welcomed to download lol)


Sean Castaway

A from-scratch homage to the 1992 Johnny Castaway screensaver, starring your dad:
bald, rectangular glasses, salt-and-pepper goatee, maroon polo, and his ball cap about
half the time. One tiny island, one palm tree, and 58 different gag vignettes that
play in shuffled order, plus a full day/night cycle, weather, ships, gulls, dolphins,
planes towing banners, and the occasional UFO.

Art style (v2)

The renderer was rebuilt: game logic still runs in the classic coordinate space, but
everything is painted at 960x540 with hand-drawn cartoon linework — ink-outlined
character with a real face (glasses, expressive brows, goatee that grows during the
right gag), shaded polo with collar/buttons/tattered hem, painted gradient skies and
sea, puffy clouds, foam lapping the island, a curved palm with leaflet fronds, and
detailed props (stone phone, treasure chest, scooter, raft, sandcastle...). It scales
pixel-perfect 2x to 1080p and 4x to 4K.

Does my dad need Python? NO.

The .scr you build is a standalone exe (a Windows screensaver is literally just
an exe with a .scr extension).

You (one time, on your PC)



download: SeanCastaway.scr 
right-click it -> Install. Windows opens Screen Saver Settings with it
selected; pick a wait time and hit OK. Done.



Test it first: right-click SeanCastaway.scr -> Test (runs fullscreen; move the
mouse to exit).
